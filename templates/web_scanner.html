A website vulnerability scanner is like a digital detective that checks websites for hidden security problems that hackers could exploit. Just like a home security system scans for open doors or windows, a website scanner looks for weak spots that could let cybercriminals sneak in and cause damage. It searches for things like unlocked gates (known as vulnerabilities) that could let hackers steal information or take control of the website.

Having a website vulnerability scanner is important because it helps website owners know if their site is safe from hackers. It's like getting a regular health checkup for your website to catch any problems early before they turn into big issues. By using this tool, website owners can keep their site protected and their visitors' information secure.
